About Us

Founded in 2018, HealTheCancer (HTC) is a Blockchain project conceived out of the need to create a much-needed support system aimed at providing financial aids, medical facilities, and scientists for cancer patients across the world through the help of millions of donors.

HTC has partnered with some top world charity/health organizations and is also working to partner with the WHO and other prominent charity boards. HealTheCancer is determined to building a world-class community of healthcare services/assistance for the cancer patients.
